ITG & TE Gyrofluid Model

Introduction

Typical tokamak plasmas are low \(\beta\) and operate in the low collisionality limit. In this regime the toroidal Ion temperature gradient driven instability or the trapped electron mode is the dominant instability. These instabilities explain turbulent transport in most of the device except maybe near the edge.
